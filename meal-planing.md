# Meal Planning

## Meal Architeccture

### Daily Goal: 2400 calories
* Breakfast: 600 cal
* Snack: 100 cal
* Lunch: 600 cal
* Snack: 100
* Dinner: 1000

### Rules

1. No food after dinner (e.g. no late night snacks).
2. Fill with vegetables or fruit
3. No meat during the week (if possible)
